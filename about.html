Our story

From a Lagos dream to a global icon

The Headies began in 2006 as the Hip Hop World Awards - the first platform built to honour Nigerian music on its own terms, by its own people. It was a statement: that the artistry pouring out of Lagos studios deserved a stage as serious as the talent on it.

In the years since, it took the name the world now knows - The Headies - and grew into the most coveted recognition an African artist can receive. Its golden statuette has been lifted by the names that now headline arenas from London to New York.

In 2022 the show crossed the Atlantic for the first time, staged in Atlanta. In 2025 it came roaring home for Back to Base. And in 2026, it takes its boldest leap yet - to Canada.

"We don't just hand out awards. We document the rise of a culture - and carry Africa to the World."
