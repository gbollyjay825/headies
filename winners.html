The launchpad

The Next Rated legacy

No award in African music carries more anticipation. The Headies' Next Rated has a habit of crowning tomorrow's superstars before the rest of the world catches on - the moment a new artist is anointed as the one to watch.

The 17th Headies handed the honour to Odumodublvck, presented on stage by Davido. The 18th will name its next - when nominations drop on June 15.
